School Lunch

Everyday, we all crave the healthy, hearty meal that our school provides us. The taste is simply exquisite. Don't you love the texture of soggy brocolli in your mouth or taste of artificial cheese? Look at the image below, doesn't that just look appetizing?

Okay. Obviously I was kidding. We see half-eaten or more likely, uneaten lunch left on cafeteria tables.

The truth is that a lot of students tend to skip school lunch either because they dislike it or they are not satified with what is being offered.

In order for school lunch to change for the entire, policies must be passed in order to revamp school lunches. This seems very unlikely in the long run and the presence of Meatless Mondays and Vegatarian Fridays only reinforces this.

So, knowing that a lot of our peers may be skipping lunch, we wanted to offer some places where they can get some food after school. Most of these places will be located near Tech.

We hope to not only just offer places to eat, but also places to possibly hang out with friends. After all, food often tastes better with others.
