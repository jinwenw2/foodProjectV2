General Information

Mekong BK is a Vietnamese restaurant that specializes in serving Vietnamese cuisine for a moderate price.

Like Xifu Food, they also offer noodle dishes called pho that are delicious. The bowls usually consist of a rich broth with rice noodles, slices of beef, and other vegetables.

Mekong BK is also about a 10 minutes walk from Tech. Instead of going towards Dekalb station, Mekong BK is located the opposite way.

This place is a bit more expensive that Xifu Food. Dishes tend to be around the $15 range so they aren't as cheap but they aren't overly expensive either.

Unlike Xifu Food, the dining area for this place is bigger. This means that it can be a great spot if you have a group of 4 or 5 people.

The wait times weren't really long and food came after about 10 minutes.



Recommendations

It wouldn't be a Vietnamese restaurant without the pho.

For this reason, we recommend trying the Class Vietnamese Beef Bones Broth Pho. The Crispy Spring Rolls are very good too.

For protein, I recommend the grilled pork chops.
