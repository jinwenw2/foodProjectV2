Fast Food is Fast Food

There's not much to say about this page. Just some locations of fast food places where you can easily stop by and get food.

These places are all located within 15 minute walk from Tech and a lot of them are near train stations so you can stop by to get something quick to eat and go.

To the right are two sandwiches from Finest Deli & Smoke Shop. The top image is a "Heart Attack" and the bottom is a "Teach Wrestling."

The halal cart may be tough to locate because there is no definite address for it but it is right outside Atlantic Terminal. It'll be hard to miss it and they even have a special discount for Tech students!

Yaya Tea has some good drinks but the lines are usually long. If you have to go to Dekalb station and it's realtively empty in there, I recommend getting their rice balls. They're a quick and cheap snack to fill you up on the way.
